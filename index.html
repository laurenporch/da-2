Your game title

  • High Concept: Help Bruce eat the fish!
  • Player Experience Goals: Light-hearted maze game to inspire laughter.
  • Genre: Maze/arcade.
  • Game play: Swim up, down, left, and right using the arrow keys to help Bruce eat all of the fish on the screen.
  • Prototype goal: Movement of the fish/trying to get random movement.
  • Features: Set underwater with a cute and hungry shark. Future updates will include eels that can hurt Bruce and make him spit a fish back out.
  • Setting/Premise: Help a hungry shark at the bottom of the ocean snack on tasty fish.
  • Story: Bruce hasn't eaten in days because the fish are so crazy! Help Bruce get to all of the fish so that he can get full and take a nap down here near the bottom of the sea.
  • Target Audience: Kids and bored adults.
  • Play time: 1-2 minutes.
  • Strategies: Clearing left-half of the screen of fish before moving to the right-half (there's only one way through the middle).
